Information We Collect

Smart Split collects only the information necessary to provide our expense sharing services:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and optional bank details
  • Group Data: Group names, descriptions, and member information
  • Expense Records: Transaction details, amounts, and receipt images you upload
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service for improvement purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide expense sharing and settlement services
  • Send notifications about group activities and payments
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Data Sharing

Your privacy is important to us. We:

  • Do not sell your personal information to third parties
  • Only share expense data with group members you've chosen to join
  • May share aggregated, anonymized data for service improvement
  • Will disclose information only when required by law

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure data transmission using industry-standard encryption
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure storage of uploaded receipt images

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold
  • Update your account information at any time
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your expense data

Data Retention

We retain your information as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide our services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

You can request deletion of your data at any time through your account settings.

Cookies and Tracking

Smart Split uses minimal tracking:

  • Essential cookies for login sessions and core functionality
  • No third-party advertising or analytics cookies
  • Local storage for app preferences and offline functionality

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of Smart Split after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: mail@stephanevdb.com
  • GitHub: stephanevdb/smart-split
  • Issues: Report privacy concerns through GitHub Issues
